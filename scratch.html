Press The Spacebar

The goal of the game is to have the cat sprite survive as long as possible by jumping over the basketballs. As you progress through the game the basketballs will roll down the hill faster. The score is increased by 1 for each jump over a basketball. The game ends when the cat sprite makes contact with a basketball.

I learned about if else statements and how you can stack structures of codes to simulate an action for a sprite.
